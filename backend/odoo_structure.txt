   <section id="convert-leads-into-opportunities">
    <h1>
     Convert leads into opportunities
     <a class="headerlink" href="#convert-leads-into-opportunities" title="Permalink to this headline">
      ¶
     </a>
    </h1>
    <p>
     <em>
      Leads
     </em>
     act as a qualifying step before an opportunity is created. This provides additional time to
review its potential, and gauge its viability, before the opportunity is assigned to a salesperson.
    </p>
   </section>
   <section id="configuration">
    <h2>
     Configuration
     <a class="headerlink" href="#configuration" title="Permalink to this headline">
      ¶
     </a>
    </h2>
    <p>
     To activate the
     <em>
      Leads
     </em>
     setting, navigate to
     <span class="menuselection">
      CRM app ‣ Configuration ‣ Settings
     </span>
     and check the box labeled,
     <span class="guilabel">
      Leads
     </span>
     . Then, click
     <span class="guilabel">
      Save
     </span>
     .
    </p>
    <img alt="Leads setting on CRM configuration page." class="align-center" src="../../../../_images/convert-leads-leads-setting.png"/>
    <p>
     Activating this feature adds a new menu option,
     <span class="guilabel">
      Leads
     </span>
     , to the header bar, located along
the top of the screen.
    </p>
    <img alt="Leads menu on CRM application." class="align-center" src="../../../../_images/convert-leads-leads-menu.png"/>
    <p>
     Once the
     <em>
      Leads
     </em>
     setting has been activated, it applies to all sales teams by default. To turn off
leads for a specific team, navigate to
     <span class="menuselection">
      CRM app ‣ Configuration ‣ Sales Teams
     </span>
     .
Then, select a team from the list to open that team’s configuration page. Clear the
     <span class="guilabel">
      Leads
     </span>
     checkbox, located beneath the
     <span class="guilabel">
      Sales Team
     </span>
     field, then click
     <span class="guilabel">
      Save
     </span>
     .
    </p>
    <img alt="Leads menu on CRM application." class="align-center" src="../../../../_images/convert-leads-leads-button.png"/>
   </section>
   <section id="convert-a-lead-into-an-opportunity">
    <h2>
     Convert a lead into an opportunity
     <a class="headerlink" href="#convert-a-lead-into-an-opportunity" title="Permalink to this headline">
      ¶
     </a>
    </h2>
    <p>
     To convert a lead into an
     <em>
      opportunity
     </em>
     , navigate to
     <span class="menuselection">
      CRM app ‣ Leads
     </span>
     , and click
on a lead from the list to open it.
    </p>
    <div class="alert alert-warning">
     <p class="alert-title">
      Warning
     </p>
     <p>
      If a
      <span class="guilabel">
       Similar Leads
      </span>
      smart button appears at the top of the page for the lead, it
indicates a similar lead or opportunity already exists in the database. Before converting this
lead, click the smart button to confirm if the lead should be merged.
     </p>
     <img alt="Close up of a lead with emphasis on the Similar Leads smart button." class="align-center" src="../../../../_images/similar-leads-smart-button.png"/>
    </div>
    <p>
     Click the
     <span class="guilabel">
      Convert to Opportunity
     </span>
     button, located at the top-left of the page.
    </p>
    <img alt="Create opportunity button on a lead record." class="align-center" src="../../../../_images/convert-leads-convert-opp-button.png"/>
    <p>
     This opens a
     <span class="guilabel">
      Convert to opportunity
     </span>
     pop-up modal. Here, in the
     <span class="guilabel">
      Conversion
Action
     </span>
     field, select the
     <span class="guilabel">
      Convert to opportunity
     </span>
     option.
    </p>
    <div class="alert alert-primary">
     <p class="alert-title">
      Note
     </p>
     <p>
      To merge this lead with an existing similar lead or opportunity, select
      <span class="guilabel">
       Merge with
existing opportunities
      </span>
      in the
      <span class="guilabel">
       Conversion Action
      </span>
      field. This generates a list of the
similar leads/opportunities to be merged.
     </p>
     <p>
      When merging, Odoo gives priority to whichever lead/opportunity was created in the system first,
merging the information into the first created lead/opportunity. However, if a lead and an
opportunity are being merged, the resulting record is referred to as an opportunity, regardless
of which record was created first.
     </p>
    </div>

     Then, select a
     <span class="guilabel">
      Salesperson
     </span>
     and a
     <span class="guilabel">
      Sales Team
     </span>
     to which the opportunity should
